Land & Property Records

JPG
Austin's Colony Register — John Thomas entry (1835)
Original page from Stephen F. Austin's Colony Register (1835), page 26 of the Families section. Shows the entry for John Thomas: "John Thomas 50 years of age Moved from Alabama / Phoebe his wife 50 years of age / 6 Male 2 female children." Primary source confirming John C. Thomas Sr. and Phoebe Springer as James Avery Thomas's parents. The same page lists neighbors John Springer, Wm. Morris, and Ezekiel Springer — all arriving together from Alabama. Marginal note: "Want land in the San Jacinto District & have gone to examine it."

Michael Moore Kennard Letter — January 4, 1861
Richard Gary Thomas's typed transcription of the letter from Michael Moore Kennard to his brother A.D. Kennard Jr., dated January 4, 1861. Covers family life and wartime hardship on the eve of the Civil War. Includes editorial notes explaining the source (an Issuu website, provided by first cousin Scott Thomas of Houston). More readable than the scanned version in the newsletter PDF. Note: This is a .docx file — it will download rather than open in the browser.
