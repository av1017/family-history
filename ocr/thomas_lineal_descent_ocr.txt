PERSONS ELIGIBLE TO MEMBERSHIP

Any male person of good moral character, who is more than eighteen years of age, and a direct descendant of an ancestor who was a
loyal resident citizen of Texas prior to its annexation to the United States of America, February 19, 1846, is eligible to membership in
the Sons of the Republic of Texas.

Boys under eighteen years of age who are otherwise qualified for membership by descent may be admitted to Junior Membership in
the organization upon written application of parent or guardian. Junior members are not subject to the payment of membership dues.
Upon reaching the age of eighteen years, a junior member automatically becomes a full member upon payment of the current annual
dues.
