IVE BRIDE—An attractive bride of the season is Mrs. J. G. Thomas, pictured
in her wedding gown of handsome Chantilly lace. Mrs. Thomas is the former
Charlotte Lucille Crawley, daughter of Mrs. John E. Crawley of Longview, and the
late J.E. Crawley. Mr. Thomas is the son of Mrs. Gary Thomas of Anderson,
and the late James Gary Thomas. The wedding took place Saturday, April 10,
[1954] at Hill Cumberland Church with Rev. L. L. Thomas officiating. After a
wedding trip to California, the couple will be at home in Houma, La.
